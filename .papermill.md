## Thesis

Cipher maps unify three concepts — algebraic cipher functors, Bernoulli approximation models, and oblivious data structures — through the cipher functor, which is a proper functor on the category of monoids. The Singular Hash Map construction achieves information-theoretic optimal space complexity of `-log₂ε + μ` bits per element while providing strong privacy guarantees.
